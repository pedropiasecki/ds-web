Keyboard

/'ki:bo:d/

noun

Meaning

  • (etc) A set of keys used to operate a typewriter, computer etc.
  • A component of many instruments including the piano, organ, and harpsichord consisting of usually back and white keys that cause different tones to be produced when struck
  • A device with keys of a musical keyboard, used to control eletronic sound-producing devices which may be built into or separate from the keyboard device.

Synonyms

eletronic keyboard

verb

Meaning

  • To type on a computer keyboard.

    • "Keyboarding is the part of this job I hate the most."
